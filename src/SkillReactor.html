    What is SkillReactor?


    SkillReactor is an incubator programme for aspoiring software developers. It gives you an oppurtunity to experience real-world software engineering as practised bhy the leading tech firms.


    You will develop a fully working fullstack product at your own pace and learn about the best practices that are wudely used across the industry.

    Demo






    Learn about Agile methodology. The project is divided in to manageable tasks organized on a Kanban Board which makes it easier to complete the project.

    Features


    Learn about Agile methodology. The project is divided in to manageable tasks organized on a Kanban Board which makes it easier to complete the project.


    Expert Guidance


    Training videos from industry experts to help you conmplete the tasks and get insights on how things are done professionally.



    Step by Step Tasks


    Complete small incremental tasks to build a fully working fullstack product. It's a great way to learn how complex problems are broken down into simple tasks.



    Instant Feedback


    Your tasks are evaluated automatically using the latest code evaluation techniques which provide useful feedback for you to learn from.



    Increase Employability


    Increase your chances of employment by advertising your performances on our employers portal.


    Leadership


    Track your rankings to see how your performance compares to others.

    Cirtificate


    Receive a cirtificate upon completion of the programme. A great addition to your resume
